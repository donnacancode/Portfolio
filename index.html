Hi! I'm Donna!
Over the past two decades, I've worked hard to build a successful career in the event management industry. I figured it was the right time to completely blow up my life and try something radically different- so here I am, attempting to learn how to code.
Why not?
I've never been the person to take things apart and figure out how they work, but I've always enjoyed building things, in both the physical and metaphorical sense. While I may not be the most experienced coder, if life has taught me anything, it's that you're never too old to learn new things, and it's always a good idea to push yourself outside of your comfort zone!
When I'm not parked in front of a computer, I enjoy spending time with my husband, our dog, and our three cats. I am so excited to learn how to make websites both pretty and functional! I have two personal projects in the pipeline that I can't wait to get started on!
