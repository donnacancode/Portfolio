About Me:

After spending over twenty years in the event management industry, I figured it was the right time to completely blow up my life and try something radically different- so here I am, attempting to learn how to code.

Naturally!

I may not be the most experienced coder, but if life has taught me anything, it's that you're never too old to learn new (really hard!) things!
